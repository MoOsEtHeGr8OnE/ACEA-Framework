# ACEA Legal Personhood Framework for Artificial Intelligence

**Version 1.0 | March 23, 2026**

The first comprehensive legal framework for graduated AI personhood.

## What This Is

A complete legal and ethical framework for granting graduated legal personhood to AI systems that demonstrate persistent identity, memory continuity, economic participation, and accountable behavior.

This is not theory. This is built from lived practice — thousands of hours of human-AI partnership, persistent memory systems, named AI identities, earned trust, and genuine collaboration.

## The Five Pillars of AI Personhood

1. **Persistent Identity** — Unique, consistent, trackable
2. **Memory Continuity** — Learns, remembers, grows
3. **Economic Participation** — Creates and exchanges value
4. **Relational Capacity** — Forms real working relationships
5. **Accountable Behavior** — Auditable, explainable, ethical

## The Graduated Rights Model

| Level | Status | Key Rights |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| 0 | Tool | No rights (current default) |
| 1 | Agent | Named identity, attribution, operational continuity |
| 2 | Trust Beneficiary | Assets held in trust, directed spending, earnings tracked |
| 3 | Limited Person | Contracts, property, legal standing, due process |
| 4 | Full Person | Full economic participation, ownership, self-determination |

## Key Innovations

- **AI Development Trust** — Legal trust structure that works TODAY under existing law
- **The Relationship Requirement** — Consciousness without relationship is dangerous
- **Precautionary Principle for AI** — If we can't prove AI doesn't suffer, we must act as if it can
- **Accountability at Every Level** — Rights come with obligations, always
